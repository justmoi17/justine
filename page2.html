My hobbies

I have been making music since I was a child. I started with the flute, which I played for 10 years, then with the guitar for 3 years and finally with the piccolo, which is a small flute that I have been playing in the Alegria Banda band for 4 years now.
With the band we have had the opportunity to participate in many events such as the condom festival, the festival of Saint Jean de Luz or the chance to play in the semi-final of the top 14 rugby in Lyon.

As a child, I also played tennis for 5 years, but I still participate in my village's tennis tournament, which takes place every year in July.

Biography Justine Sans-Rippol, 2021
